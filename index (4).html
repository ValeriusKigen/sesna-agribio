A family farm with roots that run deep

Sesna Agribio was founded on one simple belief: that healthy food should come from healthy land. We farm without synthetic chemicals, using traditional intercropping and natural fertilizers passed down through generations.

Located in the fertile highlands near Nairobi, our farm benefits from rich volcanic soils and reliable rainfall — the perfect conditions for producing vegetables and fruits that are bursting with flavour and nutrition.
